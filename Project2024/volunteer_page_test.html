About us

Welcome to our wildlife rehabilitation NGO! We are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing injured and orphaned wildlife back into their natural habitats.Our team of passionate experts works tirelessly to provide round-the-clock care for a wide range of species, ensuring their well-being and eventual return to the wild.


Programs

Our programs focus on various aspects of wildlife rehabilitation, including:

  1. Animal Rescue and Rehabilitation
  2. Habitat Restoration
  3. Community Education and Outreach


Volunteering

Volunteering plays a pivotal role in our wildlife rehabilitation NGO, serving as the heartbeat of our mission to protect and care for vulnerable wildlife populations. Each volunteer embodies the spirit of compassion and dedication, offering their time, expertise, and passion to support our efforts in rehabilitating injured and orphaned animals. Whether assisting with animal care, helping with habitat restoration projects, or educating the community about wildlife conservation, volunteers are instrumental in our endeavor to safeguard biodiversity and promote environmental stewardship. Together, we work hand in hand, united by our shared commitment to providing a second chance for wildlife and ensuring a harmonious coexistence between humans and nature.
