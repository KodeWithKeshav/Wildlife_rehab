About Us

Welcome to our wildlife rehabilitation NGO! We are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing injured and orphaned wildlife back into their natural habitats.

Our team of passionate experts works tirelessly to provide round-the-clock care for a wide range of species, ensuring their well-being and eventual return to the wild.

Programs

Our programs focus on various aspects of wildlife rehabilitation, including:

  • Animal Rescue and Rehabilitation
  • Habitat Restoration
  • Community Education and Outreach

Volunteer

Join us in making a difference for wildlife! As a volunteer, you can:

  • Assist with animal care and feeding
  • Participate in habitat restoration projects
  • Educate the community about wildlife conservation
