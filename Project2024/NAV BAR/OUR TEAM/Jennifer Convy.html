HELLO

I'm JENNIFER CONVY
 a Secretary, Human Resources Committee Chair

I have over 31 years of animal welfare experience including 29 years of supervisory and management experience in animal care, operations, and leadership positions overseeing over fifty employees with thirteen direct reports today.
I began my career in the equine and small animal veterinary field, then pivoted to a wildlife rehabilitation career at Missouri Wildlife Rescue Center as a Wildlife Technician and Center Manager before coming to PAWS Wildlife Center in 1996.
I served as an executive Board member for the OASIS and am the Secretary and Human Resources Chairperson for OASIS. I am a founding Board member and Past President of the Washington Wildlife Rehabilitation Association. My husband and I have three busy teenage sons and three cute PAWS dogs and live in Snohomish, Washington.
