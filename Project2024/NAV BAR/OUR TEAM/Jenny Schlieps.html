HELLO

I'm Jenny Schlieps
 a Publications Manager

Jenny serves as Publications Manager for OASIS.
She is a wildlife rehabilitator, specializing in oiled wildlife rehabilitation and response planning for Focus Wildlife International, with over 25 years of experience working with wildlife.
She is a founding board member and past-President of the Washington Wildlife Rehabilitation Association (WWRA) and served on the NWRA Board for 7 years until she joined the NWRA staff.
She has an undergraduate degree in Political Science and a Certificate in Applied Animal Behavior from the University of Washington.
